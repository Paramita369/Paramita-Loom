Knowledge pages turn repeated fragments into a clearer structure. They are slower than notes, broader than reviews, and meant to hold together over time.

- Use a topic hub when several pages orbit the same question.
- Use a knowledge page when a concept deserves a stable explanation.
- Use notes when the material is still too provisional to flatten into reference.
