A commonplace ledger is a holding pattern for ideas that recur often enough to deserve a stable shelf, but not yet a full guide.

Use it when:

- the same insight appears across reviews, notes, or reading logs;
- readers need a durable anchor point;
- the material benefits from accumulation before simplification.
